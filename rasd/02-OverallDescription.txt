DOMAIN ASSUMPTIONS
* The personal informations provided by the user during the registration procedure correspond to the truth
* A safe-area is an area owned by the company with a finite number of parking slots
* Registered users won’t drive the cars if their driving license is no more valid after the registration procedure
* A registered user unabled to exploit an existing reservation of a car cannot let another registered user to use that reserved car
* The GPS signals are not altered, they’re always right
* Every car has a GPS module that can never be turned off
* Every car has a 3G module that cannot be turned off
* All the sensors in the car work correctly
* A car occurring in an accident will always be able to communicate to the system its “Unavailable” status
* The unique PIN received back by a user after the registration will always be known by the user
* A registered user cannot terminate a reservation in a non-safe area
* The safe areas are uniformly distributed in the city
